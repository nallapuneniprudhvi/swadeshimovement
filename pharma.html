Pharmaceuticals

India is one of the biggest producers of pharmaceutical products and a leading player in the global generics market, exporting nearly 50% of its production.

FDI equity inflows in the sector, during April 2014-March 2016, amounted to $2.25 Bn.

Major foreign investors included Abbot Asia Holdings ltd (United Kingdom), Mynlan Group B.V. (Netherlands) and Hospira Pte Limited (Singapore).
