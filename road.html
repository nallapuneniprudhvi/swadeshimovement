Road and Highways

The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways declared 2018-19 as the “year of construction”. In the first nine months of FY 2018-19, 5,759 km of roads and highways have been constructed.

As of 31 December 2018, 61,300 km length of roadways, costing more than $97.4 Bn were in progress.

Various landmark projects such as Bharatmala Pariyojana, Setu Bharatam, Chardham Mahamarg Vikas Pariyojna, Eastern Peripheral Expressway, Western Peripheral Expressway, etc. were underway in 2018.
